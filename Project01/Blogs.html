Halcyon Palms Luxury Villa Resort

Located close to the city of Orlando in Florida, the incredible Halcyon Palms Luxury Villa Resort is a safe, gated community featuring a wide selection of luxury Florida villas with pools and unique facilities, fantastic for creating family memories every year that will last a lifetime. These Florida villas for sale near Disney World are just a few minutes from other world famous tourist attractions including Universal Studios and Animal Kingdom. You can live the holiday dream every year and there’s some fantastic benefits across many of our villas, such as a no ownership cost option and even a developer buyback way above your purchase price! There’s also full management available, meaning everything is hassle-free! Each of the 4 and 7 bedroom villas available for sale include features such as en-suite bathrooms, exquisite fittings, a stunning covered swimming pool and a historic nature reserve bordering this exclusive community. These luxury villas in Florida are secure and safe within a gated community. Halcyon Palms features unique facilities in the area, including sporting facilities and an exclusive Clubhouse. With the historic 500-acre Shingle Creek Nature Reserve bordering the site, featuring stunning walking and cycling paths, there are incredible activities to suit everyone nearby..
